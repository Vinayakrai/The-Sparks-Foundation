THE SPARKS FOUNDATION

The foundation is a world of enabled and connected little minds which
not only builds future but also inspire students, help them innovate
and let them integrate to build the next generation humankind. Our
motive is to teach new ways of thinking, of innovating and solving
the problems on their own. Apart from letting the students integrate,
and help each other, it also helps them in learning from each other and
do well together. We aim to collaborate, and help students through
our programmes to inspire and integrate them, using innovative ways.
We believe children are gifts of god and any service to them is service
to god. We are committed to providing everyone and opportunity to
learn, grow and help others grow. We actively seek to partner with
organizations with similar goals, individuals, and corporates to
enhance the effectiveness of our programs.
